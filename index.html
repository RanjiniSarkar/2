DISABILITY TYPE WISE ENCYLOPEDIA

A disability is any condition that makes it more difficult for a person to do certain activities or interact with the world around them.
These conditions, or impairments, may be cognitive, developmental, intellectual, mental, physical, sensory, or a combination of multiple factors. Impairments causing disability may be present from birth or occur during a person's lifetime.
